The Lector philosophy

New ways of learning

Reading allows you to learn new words that you probably don't understand, but once you know them they will be part of your knowledge. So later when you meet them again you will not have any problem. You will have the ability to understand complex sentences. This ability is not available to everyone, most people prefer to see a simple summary of the text In this sense, we invite you not to depend only on the summaries that you can find in blogs, web pages, tutorials... There are authors who have a lot to teach, if you start reading you can learn much more.
